DestrierOS

DestrierOS is a multi-tenant operations platform for field-service teams that are trying to get scheduling, customer work, fleet visibility, training, compliance, and reporting out of disconnected tools.

The product started from an operator problem: real work was being split across paperwork apps, spreadsheets, policies, and systems that did not talk to each other. DestrierOS is the system I wish we had: one operating picture where the handoffs finally hold.

Visit DestrierOS.com

Some companies have a workhorse. DestrierOS is being built as the War Horse.

Overview

DestrierOS connects customer relations, GPS and locations, workforce scheduling, human resources, employee self-service, forms, learning, reporting, and admin controls. The goal is not to stack unrelated modules beside each other. The goal is to let work begin in one place and remain visible across the rest of the operation.

Scheduling is the first pressure point for many teams: double bookings, crews assigned without the right skills, and job details getting lost between office and field. DestrierOS addresses that wedge, then connects the records, permissions, training, compliance, and reporting behind it.

What it brings together

Customer work

CRM tools for accounts, contacts, leads, opportunities, tasks, quotes, documents, and schedule launch actions tied back to customer records.

Field visibility

GPS and location workflows for maps, devices, drivers, assets, maintenance, geofences, browser tracking, and telematics foundations.

Workforce scheduling

Employees, shifts, jobs, meetings, company hours, conflict intelligence, dispatch assist, auto-schedule recommendations, and crew changes.

People operations

HR records, onboarding, offboarding, leave, policy acknowledgements, employee self-service, manager queues, and compliance tracking.

Forms and learning

Secure forms, packet workflows, encrypted sensitive fields, training materials, quizzes, certifications, learner progress, and recertification alerts.

Reporting and control

Company-scoped permissions, saved report views, alerts, audit trails, workflow automation, and reporting across CRM, fleet, workforce, forms, and learning.

Current status

DestrierOS is a live platform with real product depth across CRM, fleet, workforce, HR, forms, learning, permissions, and reporting. It is built for multiple companies to operate in the same system while only seeing their own data.

  • Live foundation: Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Supabase Auth, Postgres, Row-Level Security, Storage, and Mapbox.
  • Security model: Company-scoped data isolation with role-based and module-level access controls.
  • Operational depth: Connected CRM, fleet, EMS scheduling, HR, forms, LMS, reports, alerts, audit trails, and workflow automation.
  • Hardware path: Telematics work is moving beyond browser check-ins toward vehicle-grade telemetry, with a Teltonika integration path underway.

Roadmap direction

The roadmap is focused on turning DestrierOS into a fuller end-to-end operating system: communications, inventory and purchasing, work orders, mobile field workflows, compliance audits, accounting, payroll integration, contract lifecycle management, and advanced analytics.

The through-line is practical: field teams should be able to create, schedule, execute, document, report on, and improve work without stitching together half a dozen systems.
