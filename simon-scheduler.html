Simon Scheduler (Work in Progress)

Simon Scheduler is a shared family calendar and smart-home-friendly task board designed for households that juggle schedules, chores, and shopping lists across multiple homes.

The project is currently in early development and not yet publicly available. I’m actively prototyping the data model and UI around real-world family use.

What it’s aiming to do

  • Shared family calendar that keeps multiple households in sync.
  • Chores and task boards that can be assigned to specific people.
  • Shopping and todo lists that stay in sync across devices.
  • A “wall display” mode for tablets / Raspberry Pi screens mounted in the kitchen or entryway.

If your family or household setup sounds similar, I’d love to have you test early builds and help shape how this works.
