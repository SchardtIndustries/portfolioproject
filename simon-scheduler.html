Simon Scheduler

Simon Scheduler is a live web app for families and households that need one calm place for calendars, chores, shopping lists, and the daily handoffs that keep everyone moving.

The web experience is available now, built for desktop, tablets, and smart-home-friendly displays. Native mobile apps are next, with iOS and Android releases planned soon.

What it helps with

  • A shared family calendar that keeps multiple households in sync.
  • Chores and task boards that can be assigned to specific people.
  • Shopping and todo lists that stay current across the household.
  • A wall display mode for tablets or Raspberry Pi screens mounted in the kitchen or entryway.

It is built around real household coordination: shared custody schedules, busy family routines, roommate houses, and anyone who needs less friction around the same recurring questions.
